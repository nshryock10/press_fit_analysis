Lamé Equation Setup

For thick-walled cylinder: p = δ·E/[2·r·(1+ν)·((r_o²+r_i²)/(r_o²-r_i²)+ν)] where p=contact pressure, δ=diametral interference, E=Young's modulus, r=nominal radius, r_o=outer radius, r_i=inner radius, ν=Poisson's ratio

Contact Pressure Calculation

Nominal case: p = 0.2×2300/[2×1.1×(1+0.37)×((3²+1²)/(3²-1²)+0.37)] = 0.2×2300/[2.2×1.37×(10/8+0.37)] = 460/[3.014×1.62] = 460/4.88 = 94.26 MPa. Adjusted for 50% contact: 23.77 MPa effective
