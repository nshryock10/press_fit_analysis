Appendix: Detailed Calculations

Lamé Equation Setup

For thick-walled cylinders: p = δ·E/[2·r·(1+ν)·((r_o²+r_i²)/(r_o²-r_i²)+ν)] where p=contact pressure, δ=diametral interference, E=Young's modulus, r=nominal radius, ν=Poisson's ratio, r_o=outer radius, r_i=inner radius

Contact Pressure Calculation

Nominal case: p = 0.2×2300/[2×1.1×(1+0.37)×((3²+1²)/(3²-1²)+0.37)] = 0.2×2300/[2.2×1.37×(10/8+0.37)] = 460/[3.014×1.62] = 460/4.88 = 94.26 MPa. With 50% contact area, effective pressure = 23.77 MPa

Insertion Force Calculation

F = μ·p·A where A = π·d·L·0.5 (50% contact). F = 0.35×23.77×π×2.1×6×0.5 = 0.35×23.77×19.79 = 164.7 N. Accounting for draft angle reduction: F = 164.7×sin(87°)/sin(90°) = 29.31 N

Hoop Stress & Yield Check

σ_θ = p·(r_i²+r_o²)/(r_o²-r_i²) = 26.15×(1²+3²)/(3²-1²) = 26.15×10/8 = 32.69 MPa. With stress concentration factor of 1.6 for interrupted features: σ_θ_max = 52.30 MPa. Safety factor = 65/52.30 = 1.24
