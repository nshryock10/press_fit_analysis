Optimization Suggestions

The current design suffers from excessive tolerance stack-up that creates unreliable retention performance, with 67% of tolerance combinations resulting in loose fits. The most impactful improvement is tolerance optimization to ensure consistent positive interference while maintaining reasonable assembly forces. The marginal safety factor of 1.31 indicates the design is near optimal stress levels, requiring careful balance between retention force and material limits.
