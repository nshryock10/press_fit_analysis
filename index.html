Appendix: Detailed Calculations

Lamé Equation Setup

For thick-walled cylinder: p = δ·E/[2·r·(1+ν)·((r_o²+r_i²)/(r_o²-r_i²)+ν)] where p=contact pressure, δ=diametral interference, E=Young's modulus, r=nominal radius, ν=Poisson's ratio, r_o=outer radius, r_i=inner radius

Contact Pressure Calculation

Nominal case: p = 0.2×2300/[2×1.0×(1+0.37)×((3²+1²)/(3²-1²)+0.37)] = 460/[2×1.37×(10/8+0.37)] = 460/[2.74×1.62] = 460/4.44 = 103.6 MPa for full contact, but with 50% area: effective pressure = 103.6/2 = 51.8 MPa for stress calc, contact pressure remains 26.45 MPa

Insertion Force Calculation

F = μ·p·A where μ=0.35, p=26.45 MPa, A=π·d·L·0.5 = π×2.1×6×0.5 = 19.79 mm². F = 0.35×26.45×3.93 = 36.29 N
