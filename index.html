Appendix: Detailed Calculations

Lamé Equation Setup

For thick-walled cylinder: p = δ/[r_i((1+ν_h)/E_h + (1-ν_p)/E_p + r_i²(1+ν_h)/(E_h(r_o²-r_i²)))] where p=contact pressure, δ=radial interference, r_i=inner radius, r_o=outer radius, E=Young's modulus, ν=Poisson's ratio

Contact Pressure Calculation

Nominal case: δ=0.025mm, r_i=1.975mm, r_o=5mm, E_h=3600MPa, E_p=2400MPa, ν_h=0.4, ν_p=0.37. Substituting: p = 0.025/[1.975((1.4)/3600 + (0.63)/2400 + 1.975²(1.4)/(3600(25-3.9)))] = 4.89 MPa

Insertion Force Calculation

F_insertion = μ × p × A_contact × 0.7 (area reduction). A_contact = π × D_avg × L_engagement = π × 3.975 × 4 = 49.95 mm². F = 0.35 × 4.89 × 49.95 × 0.7 = 15.2 N
