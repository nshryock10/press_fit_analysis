Lamé Equation Setup

For thick-walled cylinder: p = δE/[2r²(1+ν)] × [r_o²/(r_o²-r_i²)] where p=contact pressure, δ=diametral interference, E=Young's modulus, r=radius at interface, r_o=outer radius, r_i=inner radius, ν=Poisson's ratio
